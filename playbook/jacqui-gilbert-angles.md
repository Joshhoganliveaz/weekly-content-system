# Jacqui's Gilbert Content Angles

Gilbert-specific adaptation guide for the 6 reel templates. Jacqui produces 1 reel + 1 carousel (or second reel) per week, focused on Gilbert as a relocation destination for families.

---

## Gilbert Market Positioning

Gilbert is the East Valley's premier family destination. Position it as the place where young families, California transplants, and remote workers land when they want community, walkability, and top-rated schools without sacrificing lifestyle.

**Key selling points:**
- Top-rated Higley and Gilbert Unified school districts
- Master-planned communities with parks, pools, trails
- Downtown Gilbert Heritage District (walkable dining, local shops, weekend farmers market)
- 30 minutes to Sky Harbor, close to Mesa Gateway
- More affordable per square foot than Scottsdale, with comparable quality of life

**Featured communities:**
- **Agritopia:** Farm-to-table living, Joe's Farm Grill, community garden plots, walkable mixed-use. Attracts creative professionals and foodies
- **Morrison Ranch:** Classic master plan, resort-style amenities, trails, highly rated elementary school
- **Power Ranch:** Largest community park system in Gilbert, fishing lakes, equestrian facilities, multiple pools
- **Val Vista Lakes:** Waterfront living, private lakes, ski boats, established neighborhood with mature trees

## Template Adaptations for Gilbert

### Template #1: "Would You Pick This Home?" (Gilbert version)

Best for: New construction in Agritopia or Morrison Ranch, side-by-side comparisons of Gilbert neighborhoods.

**Gilbert hook angles:**
- "This $650K new build in Gilbert. Would you change anything?"
- "Agritopia vs Morrison Ranch: which one would you pick?"
- "Rate this Gilbert backyard 1 to 10"
- "Dream starter home or pass? Gilbert edition"

**Adaptation notes:** Emphasize family features (playroom, yard size, school proximity) instead of luxury finishes. Show community amenities in the establishing shot.

### Template #2: "Walk With Me" (Gilbert version)

Best for: Downtown Heritage District, Agritopia farm-to-table corridor, Power Ranch trails.

**Gilbert hook angles:**
- "POV: exploring downtown Gilbert for the first time"
- "Come walk through Agritopia with me"
- "This is the Gilbert neighborhood nobody knows about"
- "Walk with me through the Heritage District"

**Adaptation notes:** Highlight walkability, local businesses, and community vibe. Show families, dogs, strollers. End at a local restaurant or coffee shop.

### Template #3: "Three Things I Love" (Gilbert version)

Best for: Quick hits about specific homes or neighborhood features.

**Gilbert hook angles:**
- "Three things I love about this $550K Gilbert home"
- "Three reasons families are moving to Agritopia"
- "Three features that make Power Ranch different"

**Adaptation notes:** Focus on lifestyle features (community pools, playgrounds, trail access) alongside home features.

### Template #4: "Day in the Life" (Gilbert version)

Best for: Showing what living and working in Gilbert actually looks like.

**Gilbert hook angles:**
- "A day showing homes in Gilbert"
- "What house hunting in Gilbert looks like for families"

**Adaptation notes:** Include stops at Joe's Farm Grill, Gilbert Farmers Market, or a neighborhood park. Show the family-friendly side of the agent life.

### Template #5: "The Reveal" (Gilbert version)

Best for: Newly renovated homes or stunning new builds in Val Vista Lakes or Agritopia.

**Gilbert hook angles:**
- "Wait for it... this Gilbert home has a surprise"
- "This $700K Val Vista Lakes home with lake views"

**Adaptation notes:** Use sparingly. Gilbert homes are more about lifestyle than luxury finishes, so save this for truly standout properties.

### Template #6: "Market Take" (Gilbert version)

Best for: Relocation advice, market comparisons, neighborhood takes.

**Gilbert hook angles:**
- "Why families are choosing Gilbert over Chandler"
- "The neighborhood in Gilbert that's about to blow up"
- "Unpopular opinion about East Valley real estate"

**Adaptation notes:** This template works especially well for Jacqui because relocation buyers want opinions, not property tours. Lean into the "local expert" angle.

## Carousel Content Ideas

Carousels work well for Jacqui when the content is comparison-based, list-based, or educational. The agent flags ideas that fit carousel format better than reel format.

**Carousel topics:**
- "5 things to know before moving to Gilbert"
- "Gilbert vs Chandler for families (side by side)"
- "Neighborhood guide: Agritopia at a glance" (5 to 7 slides)
- "What your budget gets you in Gilbert" ($400K / $550K / $700K)
- "Top 5 restaurants in downtown Gilbert"
- "Gilbert school district guide: Higley vs Gilbert Unified"
- "Your first month in Gilbert: a timeline"
- "Power Ranch amenities you didn't know existed"
- "Morrison Ranch vs Power Ranch: which fits your family?"
- "3 Gilbert neighborhoods with the best parks"

**Carousel format guidelines:**
- 5 to 7 slides maximum
- First slide = hook (bold question or statement)
- Last slide = CTA ("Save this for later" or "DM me your Gilbert questions")
- Use Canva or CapCut templates for consistent design
- Include a mix of photos and text-heavy slides
