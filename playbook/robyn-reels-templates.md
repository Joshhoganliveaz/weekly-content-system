# Robyn's Reels Templates

6 templates extracted from the Instagram Reels Playbook. Tier 1 templates drive high engagement and should be posted 2x/week. Tier 2 templates build brand and should be posted 1x/week on rotation.

Weekly cadence: 3 reels (2 Tier 1 + 1 Tier 2), 1 to 2 home visits, approximately 45 minutes of editing.

---

## Template #1: "Would You Pick This Home?"

**Tier:** 1 (High Engagement)
**Frequency:** 2 to 3x/week (rotation with #2 and #3)
**Edit Time:** ~8 minutes

**Why it works:** Engagement questions drove 6x more comments than educational content. Questions in the hook are the strongest driver for algorithm reach at smaller account sizes.

### Hook Examples

- "This home is $1.1M in Scottsdale. Would you change anything?"
- "Rate this kitchen 1-10"
- "Would you keep this or renovate?"
- "Dream home or pass?"
- "Pick your favorite room"

### Shot List

1. Wide entry or establishing shot (hold 8 to 10 sec, trim to 4)
2. Kitchen or main living area (hold 8 to 10 sec)
3. Primary suite or bathroom (hold 8 to 10 sec)
4. Best unique feature: pool, view, fireplace (hold 8 to 10 sec)
5. Hook shot: the most striking single frame (film last, use as opener)

### Script / Text Overlay

- 0:00 "This $___K home in ______"
- 0:04 Price + key specs (beds / baths / sqft)
- End "Tell me in the comments"

### Audio

Trending audio from CapCut. No voiceover.

### Tone

Let the house speak. Minimal text. The question does the work.

### Caption Template

> This $___K home in ______ has me questioning everything. Would you change anything? Tell me below.
>
> #ScottsdaleRealEstate #GilbertAZ #DreamHome #AZHomes #HouseHunting

## Template #2: "Walk With Me"

**Tier:** 1 (High Engagement)
**Frequency:** 2 to 3x/week (rotation with #1 and #3)
**Edit Time:** ~12 minutes

**Why it works:** Neighborhood walk Reels outperform all other content. This is where personality shows. At smaller follower counts, being someone viewers want to follow matters more than showcasing a property.

### Hook Examples

- "Come walk this Scottsdale neighborhood with me"
- "POV: house hunting in Gilbert"
- "I found something cool on my walk today"
- "This is the neighborhood everyone asks me about"

### Shot List

1. Walking shot, camera following from behind or beside (10 sec)
2. Interesting home exterior or architectural detail (8 sec)
3. Local spot: coffee shop, park entrance, street scene (8 sec)
4. Second home or neighborhood feature (8 sec)
5. Sunset, mountain view, or wide neighborhood shot (8 sec)

### Script

Casual narration while walking. "I love this street because..." / "This is the kind of neighborhood where..." Keep it conversational, like talking to a friend on FaceTime.

### Audio

Soft music underneath voice.

### Tone

Natural, curious, enthusiastic without being performative. This is the "I want to work with her" content.

### Caption Template

> Took a walk through ______ and fell in love with the vibe. Which house caught your eye?
>
> #WalkWithMe #AZRealEstate #ScottsdaleLife #GilbertAZ

## Template #3: "Three Things I Love"

**Tier:** 1 (High Engagement)
**Frequency:** 2 to 3x/week (rotation with #1 and #2)
**Edit Time:** ~8 minutes

**Why it works:** Short, punchy, lowest effort. 15 to 20 second Reels get the highest completion rates, and the algorithm rewards completion over watch time at smaller account sizes. This is the fastest template to film and edit.

### Hook Examples

- "Three things I love about this $950K home"
- "Three features you won't believe at this price"
- "Three reasons this home won't last"

### Shot List

1. Feature #1 close-up detail shot (hold 8 sec, trim to 4)
2. Feature #2 close-up detail shot (hold 8 sec, trim to 4)
3. Feature #3 close-up detail shot (hold 8 sec, trim to 4)
4. Hook shot: holding up three fingers or pointing at camera (film last)

### Script

One sentence per thing. Observational, not salesy. "This kitchen island could seat eight." / "The primary closet has better lighting than most bathrooms." / "And this backyard at this price point?"

### Audio

Trending audio, fast cuts aligned to beats.

### Tone

Excited but specific. Name what makes it good. No generic superlatives.

### Caption Template

> Three things that made my jaw drop at this $___K listing in ______. Which one's your favorite?
>
> #ThreeThingsILove #AZRealEstate #ScottsdaleHomes #GilbertHomes #LuxuryLiving

## Template #4: "Day in the Life"

**Tier:** 2 (Brand Building)
**Frequency:** 1x/week on rotation with #5 and #6
**Edit Time:** ~12 minutes

**Why it works:** Builds the "know, like, trust" factor that converts followers to clients over 6 months. Harder to batch film, so it's supplemental. Film passively throughout the day; do not stage it.

### Hook Examples

- "What showing a $1M home actually looks like"
- "The part of real estate no one talks about"
- "A day in my life as a Scottsdale agent"

### Shot List

1. Morning routine or getting ready (5 sec)
2. Driving to showing or walking up to a home (5 sec)
3. Inside the home, one wow moment (5 sec)
4. Real reaction or candid moment (5 sec)
5. End of day or wrap-up (5 sec)

### Script

Casual voiceover added after, or real-time narration. Show the unglamorous parts too.

### Audio

Light background music under voice.

### Caption Template

> A real look at what my day looks like in Scottsdale real estate. It's not always glamorous, but I love it.
>
> #DayInTheLife #ScottsdaleRealEstate #RealEstateAgent #AZLife

## Template #5: "The Reveal"

**Tier:** 2 (Brand Building)
**Frequency:** 1x/week on rotation with #4 and #6
**Edit Time:** ~10 minutes

**Why it works:** Polished silent walkthroughs consistently get the lowest engagement, even at 200K+ followers. Beautiful but does not drive comments. Use only for the absolute best homes or portfolio pieces. Worth outsourcing for extra polish.

### Hook Examples

- "Wait for it..."
- "This $1.2M Scottsdale home surprised me"

### Shot List

1. Extreme close-up detail: faucet, countertop, fireplace (hold 10 sec, trim to 3)
2. Slow pull-back to reveal the room (hold 10 sec, trim to 5)
3. Walking transition to next best room (hold 10 sec, trim to 4)
4. Wide shot of best feature: pool, view, exterior (hold 10 sec, trim to 5)

### Script

No talking. Text overlays only, timed to transitions. 3 to 4 short lines max.

### Audio

Moody, slow-build instrumental. Cut transitions to beat drops.

### Caption Template

> Sometimes the house speaks for itself. This one in ______ definitely did.
>
> #HomeReveal #ScottsdaleHomes #LuxuryLiving #AZLuxury #DreamHome

## Template #6: "Market Take"

**Tier:** 2 (Brand Building)
**Frequency:** 1x/week on rotation with #4 and #5
**Edit Time:** ~10 minutes

**Why it works:** Educational content without opinion gets low engagement. Tie your take to a specific home or neighborhood. One point, strong stance, 15 to 20 seconds. Opinionated content builds authority.

### Hook Examples

- "Unpopular opinion about Scottsdale real estate"
- "The biggest mistake I see buyers making at $1M"
- "Nobody talks about this in Gilbert real estate"

### Shot List

1. Talking to camera, inside a listing as backdrop (15 to 20 sec total)
2. Optional: B-roll of the home over voiceover

### Script

One point. Strong stance. "Here's what I think..." End with "Agree or disagree?" or "Am I wrong?"

### Audio

Optional, low background music.

### Caption Template

> Hot take: [your opinion here]. Agree or disagree? Drop it in the comments.
>
> #RealEstateTips #ScottsdaleRealEstate #AZMarket #HotTake
