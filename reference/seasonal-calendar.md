# Seasonal Content Calendar

Monthly content angles for Scottsdale and Gilbert. The agent reads this file to adapt weekly content briefs to the current season.

---

## Spring (March through May)

**Overall themes:** Move-up buyers, new construction, outdoor living, spring landscaping

### March
- **Scottsdale:** Spring training visitors discovering Scottsdale lifestyle. Pool season starts. "Would you leave after spring training?"
- **Gilbert:** Spring break family activities. Heritage District outdoor dining. New build communities releasing spring inventory
- **Content angle:** Transition from snowbird season to local buyer momentum

### April
- **Scottsdale:** Peak outdoor living content. Golden hour patios, mountain views before summer heat. Open house season
- **Gilbert:** Agritopia spring harvest, community garden content. School district enrollment deadlines
- **Content angle:** "Last chance before summer" urgency for buyers who want to move before school starts

### May
- **Scottsdale:** Pool home showcase season. Memorial Day lifestyle content. Pre-summer price positioning
- **Gilbert:** Graduation and family milestone content. Summer camp and community pool openings
- **Content angle:** "Pool season is here" and summer-ready homes

---

## Summer (June through August)

**Overall themes:** Pool homes, mountain views at golden hour, monsoon season, summer entertaining

### June
- **Scottsdale:** Golden hour filming (early morning or late evening only). Luxury pool features. "How Scottsdale handles summer"
- **Gilbert:** Family pool community content. Indoor features that matter in summer (great room layout, kitchen islands, game rooms)
- **Content angle:** Film early morning or sunset. Position AC, insulation, and shade as selling features

### July
- **Scottsdale:** Monsoon season drama shots (sunsets, lightning, desert bloom). Summer entertaining spaces
- **Gilbert:** Monsoon content from Power Ranch lakes. Family staycation lifestyle in master-planned communities
- **Content angle:** Monsoon sunsets are the most shareable Arizona content of the year. Film every storm

### August
- **Scottsdale:** Back-to-school transition. "Prices dip in summer" market positioning. Last of the monsoon magic
- **Gilbert:** School starts. Neighborhood walkability matters most. "Which Gilbert school zone?" content
- **Content angle:** Families making fall decisions. Highlight school proximity and family features

---

## Fall (September through November)

**Overall themes:** Snowbird season, relocation content, football/tailgate lifestyle, fall market data

### September
- **Scottsdale:** First comfortable outdoor weather. Fall market picks up. Luxury buyers return from summer travel
- **Gilbert:** College football culture. Fall sports leagues at community parks. Perfect filming weather returns
- **Content angle:** "The weather just turned and the market is moving." Fresh energy after summer slowdown

### October
- **Scottsdale:** Snowbirds start arriving. Relocation content peaks. Desert hiking and outdoor lifestyle
- **Gilbert:** Fall festivals (Agritopia farm events, Heritage District festivals). Trunk-or-treat at master-plan communities
- **Content angle:** Relocation hooks work strongest October through December. "POV: you flew to Phoenix and never left"

### November
- **Scottsdale:** Holiday entertaining spaces. Year-end luxury market data. Gift guide for homeowners
- **Gilbert:** Thanksgiving at home content. Family-sized dining rooms and outdoor entertaining. Black Friday market positioning
- **Content angle:** "Holiday hosting in your dream home" and early year-end market recaps

---

## Winter (December through February)

**Overall themes:** Holiday homes, year-in-review market data, winter visitors, New Year goals

### December
- **Scottsdale:** Holiday lights and decorated luxury homes. Year-in-review market stats. "Best of" annual compilations
- **Gilbert:** Community holiday events (Morrison Ranch luminarias, downtown Gilbert lights). Family holiday content
- **Content angle:** Emotional, aspirational content. "Imagine waking up here on Christmas morning"

### January
- **Scottsdale:** New Year "fresh start" content. Snowbird population peaks. Super Bowl buzz. Winter is the best weather
- **Gilbert:** New Year family goals. "This is the year you move to Arizona" relocation hooks. Spring training preview
- **Content angle:** Goal-setting and relocation. Peak engagement for "thinking about moving to AZ" content

### February
- **Scottsdale:** Super Bowl (when applicable), spring training arrivals. Valentine's luxury lifestyle content
- **Gilbert:** Valentine's neighborhood date night guides. Spring inventory previews. "What's coming to market in Gilbert this spring"
- **Content angle:** Love-themed lifestyle content, transition from winter visitors to spring market energy
